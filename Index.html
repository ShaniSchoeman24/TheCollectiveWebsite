My Portfolio

Visual Art

Ombre Painted Arm Chairs

I specialise in painting antique furniture using techniques like chalk paint for a smooth, matte finish, gilding for beautiful gold accents, and oil paint for rich colors. One of my favorite effects is creating a sunset appearance using yellow, orange, and purple hues, combining them flawlessly to convert antique furniture into vivid pieces of art that capture the beauty of a sunset.

Visual Art

Blue Wingback Chair

The client requested that wingback chairs be painted with green and blue chalk paint to give them a new, elegant appearance. I began by cleaning and preparing the chair for a smooth surface. I then applied multiple coats of green and blue chalk paint, mixing the colours for depth and character, and meticulously sanding between layers for a perfect finish. Finally, I applied a protective wax or varnish to preserve the paint and highlight the vibrant colours.

Visual Art

Blended Ombre Sidebboard

I begin with a rich green at the bottom and blend into a vibrant blue at the top for a seamless gradient effect. Each layer of paint is applied with a brush for a textured, matte finish, resulting in a beautifully unique piece that adds artistic elegance to any room.

UI & UX

Family Cash Uber Eats App

I introduced a feature to the Uber Eats app called "Family Cash" to help elderly individuals who struggle with using digital applications. This feature allows family members to transfer money directly to a shared Uber Eats family account, ensuring that their senior loved ones have access to meals without needing to use the app themselves. "Family Cash" streamlines the ordering procedure for seniors and gives peace of mind to their family, boosting support and caring within families by making meal access more inclusive and simple.

Photography

Light Painting

In a darkened room, I utilised LED lights and a slow shutter speed on my camera to catch a mesmerising red ring. By moving the LED in a circular motion, the slow shutter speed brilliantly documented the movement, resulting in a remarkable and ethereal image.

Photography

Window Light Portraits

To capture a photo using window light, I positioned my subject near a window with ample natural light. I adjusted the angle and distance to control how the light fell on the subject, aiming for a soft and diffused effect. This approach resulted in flattering, natural illumination that enhanced details and ' textures, with gentle contrast. By carefully composing and framing the shot, I balanced light and shadows to create a visually appealing image with a harmonious blend of highlights and shadows.

Photography

Food Photography

I managed to capture in my photos the allure of just-baked brownies topped with bright blueberries and a light dusting of caster sugar. With the contrasted hues and textures of the juicy blueberries and the powdered sugar, each picture captures the decadent charm of these sweets. My goal was to evoke the visual delight and tempting temptation of these delicious brownies through careful composition and lighting, enabling viewers to savour every moment through their screens.
